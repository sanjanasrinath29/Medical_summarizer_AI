TEST: TSH
UNIT: mIU/L
NORMAL: 0.4 - 4.0
LOW_MEANS: TSH is low. May indicate hyperthyroidism. Symptoms include weight loss and rapid heartbeat.
HIGH_MEANS: TSH is high. May indicate hypothyroidism. Symptoms include fatigue and weight gain.

TEST: T3
UNIT: ng/dL
NORMAL: 80 - 200
LOW_MEANS: T3 is low. May indicate hypothyroidism.
HIGH_MEANS: T3 is high. May indicate hyperthyroidism.

TEST: T4
UNIT: mcg/dL
NORMAL: 5.0 - 12.0
LOW_MEANS: T4 is low. May indicate hypothyroidism.
HIGH_MEANS: T4 is high. May indicate hyperthyroidism.
