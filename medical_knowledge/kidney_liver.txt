TEST: CREATININE
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL_MALE: 0.74 - 1.35
NORMAL_FEMALE: 0.59 - 1.04
HIGH_MEANS: Creatinine is high. May indicate kidney dysfunction.

TEST: UREA/BUN
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: 7 - 20
HIGH_MEANS: Urea is high. Kidneys may not be filtering waste properly.

TEST: URIC ACID
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL_MALE: 3.4 - 7.0
NORMAL_FEMALE: 2.4 - 6.0
HIGH_MEANS: Uric acid is high. May indicate gout or kidney issues.

TEST: SGPT/ALT
UNIT: U/L
NORMAL: 7 - 56
HIGH_MEANS: SGPT is high. Liver may be inflamed or damaged.

TEST: SGOT/AST
UNIT: U/L
NORMAL: 10 - 40
HIGH_MEANS: SGOT is high. May indicate liver or heart muscle damage.

TEST: BILIRUBIN TOTAL
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: 0.2 - 1.2
HIGH_MEANS: Bilirubin is high. May indicate jaundice or liver disease.
