TEST: ALKALINE PHOSPHATASE (ALP)
UNIT: U/L
NORMAL: 44 - 147
LOW_MEANS: ALP is low. Rarely significant.
HIGH_MEANS: ALP is high. May indicate liver disease, bile duct obstruction, or bone disease.

TEST: GGT (GAMMA GLUTAMYL TRANSFERASE)
UNIT: U/L
NORMAL_MALE: 8 - 61
NORMAL_FEMALE: 5 - 36
HIGH_MEANS: GGT is high. Sensitive marker for liver stress. Often elevated with alcohol use or fatty liver.

TEST: TOTAL PROTEIN
UNIT: g/dL
NORMAL: 6.0 - 8.3
LOW_MEANS: Total protein is low. May indicate malnutrition or liver disease.
HIGH_MEANS: Total protein is high. May indicate dehydration or infection.

TEST: ALBUMIN
UNIT: g/dL
NORMAL: 3.5 - 5.0
LOW_MEANS: Albumin is low. Indicates poor nutrition or liver not functioning well.
HIGH_MEANS: Albumin is high. Usually indicates dehydration.

TEST: GLOBULIN
UNIT: g/dL
NORMAL: 2.0 - 3.5
HIGH_MEANS: Globulin is high. May indicate infection, inflammation, or immune disorder.

TEST: A/G RATIO (ALBUMIN/GLOBULIN)
UNIT: Ratio
NORMAL: 1.2 - 2.2
LOW_MEANS: A/G ratio is low. May indicate liver disease or immune disorder.

TEST: DIRECT BILIRUBIN
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: 0.0 - 0.3
HIGH_MEANS: Direct bilirubin is high. May indicate bile duct obstruction or liver disease.

TEST: INDIRECT BILIRUBIN
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: 0.2 - 0.9
HIGH_MEANS: Indirect bilirubin is high. May indicate hemolysis or Gilbert syndrome.
