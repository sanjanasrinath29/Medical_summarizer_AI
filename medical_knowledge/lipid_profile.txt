TEST: TOTAL CHOLESTEROL
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: Below 200
BORDERLINE: 200 - 239
HIGH: 240 and above
HIGH_MEANS: Total cholesterol is high. Risk of heart disease increases.

TEST: LDL (Bad Cholesterol)
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: Below 100
BORDERLINE: 100 - 129
HIGH: 130 and above
HIGH_MEANS: LDL is high. Increases risk of artery blockage and heart attack.

TEST: HDL (Good Cholesterol)
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL_MALE: Above 40
NORMAL_FEMALE: Above 50
LOW_MEANS: HDL is low. Lower protection against heart disease.

TEST: TRIGLYCERIDES
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: Below 150
BORDERLINE: 150 - 199
HIGH: 200 - 499
VERY_HIGH: 500 and above
HIGH_MEANS: Triglycerides are high. Risk of heart disease and pancreatitis.
TEST: HDL/LDL RATIO
UNIT: Ratio
NORMAL: 0.3 - 0.4
HIGH_MEANS: HDL to LDL ratio is high. This actually means more good cholesterol relative to bad cholesterol. Generally considered favorable but discuss with doctor.

TEST: TOTAL CHOLESTEROL/HDL RATIO
UNIT: Ratio
NORMAL: Below 4.0
BORDERLINE: 4.0 - 5.0
HIGH_MEANS: Total cholesterol to HDL ratio is high. Higher risk of heart disease.

TEST: VLDL CHOLESTEROL
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: Below 30
HIGH_MEANS: VLDL is high. Associated with increased triglycerides and heart disease risk.

TEST: NON-HDL CHOLESTEROL
UNIT: mg/dL
NORMAL: Below 130
HIGH_MEANS: Non-HDL cholesterol is high. Includes all bad cholesterol types.

TEST: HbA1c IFCC
UNIT: mmol/mol
NORMAL: Below 39.0
HIGH_MEANS: HbA1c IFCC is high. Indicates poor blood sugar control over past 3 months.

TEST: HS CRP (HIGH SENSITIVITY CRP)
UNIT: mg/L
NORMAL: Below 1.0
BORDERLINE: 1.0 - 3.0
HIGH: Above 3.0
HIGH_MEANS: HS CRP is high. Indicates inflammation in the body. May signal infection, heart disease risk, or autoimmune condition.

TEST: ESR (ERYTHROCYTE SEDIMENTATION RATE)
UNIT: mm/hr
NORMAL_FEMALE: Below 20
NORMAL_MALE: Below 15
HIGH_MEANS: ESR is high. Indicates inflammation or infection somewhere in the body.

TEST: MCHC
UNIT: g/dL
NORMAL: 31.5 - 34.5
LOW_MEANS: MCHC is low. Red blood cells have less hemoglobin concentration than normal. Consistent with iron deficiency anemia.

TEST: PCV (PACKED CELL VOLUME / HEMATOCRIT)
UNIT: %
NORMAL_MALE: 40 - 50
NORMAL_FEMALE: 36 - 46
LOW_MEANS: PCV is low. Indicates reduced red blood cell volume. Consistent with anemia.

TEST: TLC (TOTAL LEUCOCYTE COUNT)
UNIT: 10^9/L
NORMAL: 4.0 - 10.0
LOW_MEANS: TLC is low. Reduced white blood cells. May indicate viral infection or immune issue.
HIGH_MEANS: TLC is high. Elevated white blood cells. May indicate infection or inflammation.

TEST: PLATELET COUNT
UNIT: 10^9/L
NORMAL: 150 - 410
LOW_MEANS: Platelets are low. Risk of bleeding.
HIGH_MEANS: Platelets are high. Risk of clotting.

TEST: HBsAg (HEPATITIS B SURFACE ANTIGEN)
UNIT: Qualitative
NORMAL: Negative
HIGH_MEANS: HBsAg is positive. Indicates Hepatitis B infection. Consult doctor immediately.
